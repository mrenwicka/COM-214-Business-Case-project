Weezer Biography :3

Weezer is an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1992. The band's lineup consists of Rivers Cuomo, Patrick Wilson, Brian Bell, and Scott Shriner.

Weezer has released several critically acclaimed albums, including their debut self-titled album, also known as the Blue Album, and Pinkerton.
